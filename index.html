About Me

Welcome to my corner of the digital realm! I'm Dhiraj Raut, a driven Software Quality Assurance Engineer with a passion for perfecting the user experience. With over 18 months of hands-on experience in the field, I've honed my skills across various testing methodologies, including manual, API, and automation testing.

Join me on this journey as we embark on a quest to elevate the standard of quality in the digital landscape, one meticulously tested line of code at a time.
